APU Fitness Hub ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Full name, email, phone number, date of birth
  • Health Information: Fitness goals, medical conditions (voluntarily provided)
  • Payment Information: Credit card details (processed via Stripe)
  • Usage Data: Website interaction, class bookings, equipment usage

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us to:

  • Create and manage your membership account
  • Process payments and send renewal reminders
  • Improve our services and website experience
  • Send important updates and fitness tips
  • Customize workout plans and class recommendations
  • Send promotional offers (opt-out available)
  • Improve facility safety and service quality
  • Personalize your workout recommendations

3. Data Sharing

We only share information with:

  • Certified personal trainers
  • Payment processing partners (Stripe, PayPal)
  • Government authorities when required by law

4. Data Security

We implement:

  • SSL encryption for all transactions
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited staff access to sensitive data

5. Your Rights

  • Access personal data we hold
  • Request data correction
  • Withdraw consent for marketing
  • Request account deletion

7. Changes to Terms

We reserve the right to modify these terms at any time. Continued use of services constitutes agreement to updated terms.
