About APU Fitness Hub

Your Campus Gateway to Health, Wellness, and Peak Performance

Our Story

Established in 2020, APU Fitness Hub began as a student initiative to promote health and wellness on campus. What started as a small gym with 3 cardio machines has grown into a 5,000 sq. ft. fitness complex serving 2,000+ students annually.
