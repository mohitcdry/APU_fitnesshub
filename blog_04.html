Brain Food: What to Eat During Exam Season

Boost your focus with these power foods

Published on: March 20, 2024 | 5 min read

Exams are stressful, and your brain needs fuel to perform at its best. Forget the instant noodles—here’s what to eat to ace your study sessions.

1. Blueberries for Memory

Packed with antioxidants, blueberries improve memory retention. Snack on a handful or toss them into yogurt.

2. Dark Chocolate for Focus

Caffeine and flavonoids in dark chocolate (70%+ cocoa) sharpen concentration. A small square is enough—no sugar overload!

Pair it with nuts for a brain-boosting combo.

3. Eggs for Energy

Eggs are rich in choline, which supports brain cell function. Boil a few for a quick breakfast or snack.

4. Omega-3 Power

Salmon or sardines boost cognitive skills. On a budget? Try flaxseeds in smoothies.

Quick Meal Prep

Try this: scrambled eggs with spinach (10 mins), or a tuna salad with whole-grain bread (15 mins). Prep ahead to save time.

Eating smart during exams isn’t just about passing—it’s about thriving. Stock your pantry with these, and power through!
