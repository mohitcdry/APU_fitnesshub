Healthy Meals Under RM15

Eat well without breaking the bank

Published on: March 12, 2024 | 6 min read

Student budgets are tight, but that doesn’t mean you can’t eat healthy. With local Malaysian ingredients, here’s how to whip up nutritious meals for under RM15.

1. Nasi Goreng Kampung

Cost: ~RM8. Fry leftover rice with anchovies, kangkung, and an egg. Packed with protein and greens!

2. Chicken Stir-Fry

Cost: ~RM10. Use chicken breast (RM5), mixed veggies (RM3), and soy sauce (RM2). Serve with rice.

Buy veggies from wet markets—they’re fresher and cheaper!

3. Egg and Tofu Combo

Cost: ~RM6. Scramble eggs (RM2) with tofu (RM2) and spinach (RM2). Quick, cheap, and filling.

Shopping Smart

Stock up on rice, eggs, and frozen veggies in bulk. Shop at local pasar malam for deals on chicken and fish.

Healthy eating on a budget is all about creativity. These meals prove you can nourish your body without emptying your wallet.
